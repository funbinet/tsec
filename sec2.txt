## 1. RECONNAISSANCE

### Phase Overview
The Reconnaissance phase focuses on gathering intelligence about target organizations, employees, infrastructure, and digital footprint through open-source intelligence (OSINT) techniques. This phase is critical for identifying attack vectors and potential entry points that can be leveraged in later stages.
